About AL1 DAO

AL1 DAO (Axes Labs One) is a founder-led, tokenized organization driving innovation at the intersection of crypto, AI, and financial technology. Structured as a decentralized autonomous organization, AL1 brings together a powerful network of builders, investors, and advisors to launch category-defining products.

We operate on-chain, make decisions through governance, and reward contribution with AXES — our native governance token.

What We're Building

A crypto-native fintech platform combining seamless user experiences for on-chain finance, AI-powered tools for users and protocols, and real revenue capture with tokenholder upside.

AXES Token

The AXES Token represents ownership, voice, and upside in AL1 DAO. It's a governance token for voting, a value-accrual asset tied to revenues, and a rights token for future allocations.
